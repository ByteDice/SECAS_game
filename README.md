# Super Cool Epic Awesome Stuff: The Game
A game made for the LBS gymnasium game-jam #2 (2025).\
(Duration: 42 hours. Theme: "British Pigs")

> [!NOTE]
> This will remain **untouched** from what I made during the jam.
> Things may be too easy, too hard, and/or confusing.
> Expect a buggy mess. It was my first ever jam.

## How to play:
Download the source and open it in Godot (4.5).

The goal is to protect your pigs from seagulls by feeding them (the seagulls) biscuits. \
You gain biscuits from the *Biscuit o' tron v9000*'s by inputting a sequence of directions (WASD / arrow keys) and can carry up to 3 at once. \
Moving is done with the mouse.
